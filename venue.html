Registration

The registration to the conference is available through the University of Bradford online store.

  • Early bird fee (until 1 July 2017): £240
  • Full registration fee (from 2 July until 25 July 2017): £270

The conference venue

The conference will be hosted by DHEZ (Digital Health Enterprise Zone) and will take place in Digital Exchange Building, conveniently located in the centre of Bradford, 34 Peckover St, BD1 5BD.

.

Built in 1867 and originally a railway hotel, the Great Victoria is a landmark Victorian building in the heart of the vibrant and exciting city of Bradford. Over the years, it has been a favourite haunt for wealthy merchants, stars of the stage, rock & pop and political leaders. Today it is one of the most stylish and contemporary hotels in Bradford with 70 super chic, individually styled luxury bedrooms and suites, the ever popular Vic & Berties Restaurant & Grill and Berties Bar. The hotel is opposite Bradford Interchange train station, within walking distance to Bradford's famous theatres, the National Media Museum and all that the beautiful Yorkshire countryside has to offer.

How to find us

Bradford is conveniently situated in the centre of the UK and is easily reached by road, rail or air. The closest airports are Leeds Bradford Airport and Manchester Airport. The city is connected to the national motorway network via the M62 (junction 26) and the M606.
